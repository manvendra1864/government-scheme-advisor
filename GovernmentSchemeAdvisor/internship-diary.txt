📘 Internship Diary: Week 01  
In the first week of my internship, I was introduced to the Government Scheme Advisor project (My Yojana). The objective was to build a web platform to help users discover government welfare schemes tailored to their needs. I began by understanding the project requirements and exploring the existing codebase, which uses Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, and SQLite. I set up the development environment, installed dependencies, and ran the application locally. Initial challenges included resolving circular import errors and configuring the database connection. With guidance from documentation and online resources, I successfully launched the server and accessed the homepage. This week gave me a solid foundation in the project’s architecture and workflow.

📘 Internship Diary: Week 03
